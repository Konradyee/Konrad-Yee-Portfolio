Aguas Calientes Suspended Bridge

Project Overview

A 65.5 m pedestrian footbridge crossing Rio Blanco in the Ácora municipality of Puno, Peru. Designed to provide safe year-round passage for approximately 300 community members cut off during seasonal flooding, connecting them to schools, markets, and essential services.

The design features custom asymmetric abutments — a 2G-80A on the left bank and 1G-80A on the right — optimized to minimize excavation on difficult rocky terrain while satisfying all safety factors of the 2025 EIA Bridge Binder.
